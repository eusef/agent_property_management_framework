# Seasonal Maintenance Guide

**Purpose:** Property maintenance tasks organized by season. Proactive seasonal maintenance prevents expensive emergency repairs and extends the life of major systems.
**Integration:** These tasks should be added to `compliance/calendar.md` with specific dates based on your climate zone.

---

## Spring (March - May)

| Task | Priority | Frequency | Notes |
|------|----------|-----------|-------|
| HVAC A/C servicing | HIGH | Annual | Service before cooling season. Replace filters. Schedule with HVAC vendor. |
| Exterior inspection | HIGH | Annual | Walk the entire property: siding, foundation, trim, windows, doors. Note any winter damage. |
| Gutter cleaning | HIGH | Semi-annual | Clear debris from fall/winter. Check for damage or sagging. |
| Test sprinkler system | MEDIUM | Annual | Turn on, check all heads, adjust coverage, check for leaks. |
| Inspect roof | HIGH | Annual | Look for missing/damaged shingles, flashing issues, moss growth. Schedule repairs before rain season. |
| Inspect deck/patio | MEDIUM | Annual | Check for rot, loose boards, popping nails. Seal or stain if needed. |
| Clean dryer vent | HIGH | Annual | Fire prevention. Professional cleaning recommended. |
| Test smoke/CO detectors | HIGH | Semi-annual | Replace batteries. Replace units older than 10 years. |
| Inspect weather stripping | MEDIUM | Annual | Replace worn weather stripping on doors and windows. |
| Pressure wash | LOW | Annual | Driveway, walkways, siding. Improves curb appeal and prevents buildup. |
| Landscape cleanup | MEDIUM | Annual | Trim bushes, clear debris, mulch beds, check irrigation. |

---

## Summer (June - August)

| Task | Priority | Frequency | Notes |
|------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Clean A/C filters | HIGH | Monthly (tenant) | Remind tenants or provide filters. Dirty filters reduce efficiency and cause breakdowns. |
| Inspect exterior paint | MEDIUM | Annual | Look for peeling, cracking, or fading. Plan repainting for dry weather. |
| Check caulking (exterior) | MEDIUM | Annual | Windows, doors, trim. Re-caulk where deteriorated. |
| Inspect trees and landscaping | MEDIUM | Annual | Dead branches, overgrown trees near roof/power lines. Schedule trimming. |
| Check outdoor faucets and hoses | LOW | Annual | Look for leaks, replace washers. |
| Pest inspection | MEDIUM | Annual | Look for evidence of ants, termites, rodents. Schedule treatment if needed. |

---

## Fall (September - November)

| Task | Priority | Frequency | Notes |
|------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Furnace inspection and servicing | HIGH | Annual | Service before heating season. Replace filter. Check for CO leaks. |
| Gutter cleaning (post leaf-drop) | HIGH | Semi-annual | Critical before rain/snow season. Clogged gutters cause foundation and water damage. |
| Winterize outdoor faucets | HIGH | Annual | Disconnect hoses, insulate outdoor spigots, shut off exterior water lines where possible. |
| Inspect weather stripping (doors/windows) | MEDIUM | Annual | Gaps waste energy and allow moisture intrusion. |
| Check insulation | MEDIUM | Every 2-3 years | Attic and crawlspace. Adequate insulation prevents frozen pipes and reduces heating costs. |
| Chimney inspection/cleaning | MEDIUM | Annual (if applicable) | If the property has a wood-burning fireplace. |
| Test smoke/CO detectors | HIGH | Semi-annual | Replace batteries. Replace units older than 10 years. |
| Inspect roof | HIGH | Annual | Before rain/snow season. Address any issues found in spring inspection. |
| Clear yard debris | MEDIUM | As needed | Falling leaves, dead branches. Prevent drainage issues. |

---

## Winter (December - February)

| Task | Priority | Frequency | Notes |
|------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Monitor for frozen pipes | HIGH | Ongoing | If temps drop below freezing. Remind tenants to keep heat at minimum 55F. Open cabinet doors under sinks on exterior walls. |
| Snow/ice removal | HIGH | As needed | Clear walkways and driveways. Liability risk if someone slips on your property. |
| Check heating system performance | MEDIUM | Monthly | Confirm tenant is changing filters. Respond quickly to heating complaints (habitability requirement). |
| Inspect for ice dams | MEDIUM | As needed | Look for icicle buildup on roof edges. Can cause water intrusion. |
| Check for drafts | LOW | Annual | Walk the unit and feel for drafts around windows and doors. |
| Plan spring maintenance | LOW | Once | Review this year's maintenance log, plan improvements for spring. |

---

## Climate-Specific Adjustments

Adjust the calendar based on your property locations:

| Climate | Key Adjustments |
|---------|----------------|
| Hot/dry (AZ, TX, NV) | Focus on A/C maintenance. Less concern about frozen pipes. Add: inspect for foundation cracks from soil expansion. |
| Cold/snow (MN, WI, CO) | Heavy focus on winterization. Longer heating season maintenance. Snow removal is a major ongoing task. |
| Humid/coastal (FL, SC, LA) | Mold prevention is critical. Hurricane preparedness. Pest pressure is year-round. More frequent exterior inspections. |
| Pacific Northwest (OR, WA) | Heavy rain focus. Moss/mildew treatment. Gutter maintenance is critical. Less A/C concern. |
