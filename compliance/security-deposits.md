## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Missing the deadline.** Set a calendar reminder the day of move-out. The clock starts immediately.
2. **Not itemizing.** A lump-sum deduction without itemization may be unenforceable.
3. **Deducting for normal wear and tear.** When in doubt, don't deduct. Courts tend to side with tenants on borderline items.
4. **No move-in inspection.** Without documentation of move-in condition, proving damage at move-out is very difficult.
5. **Commingling funds.** Keep deposits in a separate trust account. Period.
6. **Not accounting for depreciation.** If your state requires it, you cannot charge full replacement cost for a worn item.
