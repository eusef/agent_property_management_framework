# Vacancy Marketing Guide

**Purpose:** How to find qualified tenants quickly when you have a vacancy. The goal is to minimize vacancy days while attracting the highest quality applicants.
**Key principle:** Marketing is a system, not a one-time ad. Build repeatable processes.

---

## When to Start Marketing

Start advertising **immediately** when you receive a move-out notice. Every day of overlap between the departing and incoming tenant reduces vacancy cost.

**Timeline:**
- Day 1 (notice received): Post listings, prepare the unit for showings (if possible while occupied)
- Ongoing: Process inquiries through the pre-screening funnel (see `templates/checklists/pre-screening-funnel.md`)
- Move-out day: Begin unit turnover (see `templates/checklists/unit-turnover.md`)
- Goal: New tenant moves in within 1-2 weeks of previous tenant vacating

## Advertising Channels

### Free Channels (Start Here)

| Channel | Best For | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Zillow Rental Manager | Broadest reach | Free listing. Auto-syndicates to Trulia and HotPads. |
| Facebook Marketplace | Local reach | Large audience, easy to post, good for quick fills. |
| Craigslist | Budget-conscious tenants | Still high-traffic in most markets. Use cross-streets, not exact address. |
| Yard sign | Drive-by traffic | Place a professional sign with a dedicated phone number. Cost: ~$20. |
| Existing tenant referrals | Highest quality leads | Offer a $100 referral bonus to current tenants. Best tenants often know other good tenants. |

### Paid Channels (If Needed)

| Channel | Cost | When to Use |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Apartments.com | Varies | If free channels aren't generating enough inquiries after 2 weeks |
| Local newspaper | Varies | Older demographics, certain markets |
| Realtor MLS listing | Agent fee | High-end properties or competitive markets |

## Listing Copy Best Practices

### Do Include
- Monthly rent amount
- Security deposit amount
- Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
- Square footage
- Key amenities (parking, laundry, yard, storage)
- Pet policy (allowed/not allowed, restrictions, fees)
- Minimum income requirement (3x monthly rent)
- Lease term
- Available date
- Application process and fee
- Equal Housing Opportunity statement

### Do Not Include
- Anything that references a preferred tenant by protected class (see `compliance/fair-housing.md`)
- "Perfect for young professionals," "quiet community," "ideal family home"
- Your home address or personal phone number

### Sample Listing Structure

**[Property Type] for Rent - [Beds]bd/[Baths]ba - $[Rent]/month**

[Brief property description - 2-3 sentences highlighting best features]

**Details:**
- Bedrooms: [X]
- Bathrooms: [X]
- Square feet: [X]
- Available: [Date]
- Lease: [Term]
- Deposit: $[Amount]
- Pets: [Policy]

**Requirements:** Minimum household income of $[3x Rent]/month (verifiable). Credit and background check required. Application fee: $[Amount].

**Contact:** [Business phone] or [Business email]

Equal Housing Opportunity

## Photography Guidelines

Good photos are the single biggest factor in generating inquiries. Bad photos cause prospects to skip your listing.

**Before shooting:**
- Clean the unit thoroughly
- Remove all clutter and personal items
- Open all blinds and curtains (natural light is essential)
- Turn on all lights
- Stage minimally if vacant (a few simple items can help prospects visualize the space)

**Shooting tips:**
- Shoot on a bright day
- Use landscape orientation (horizontal)
- Stand in the doorway of each room and shoot toward the opposite corner
- Capture each room from at least 2 angles
- Include exterior shots (front, back, yard, parking)
- Include shots of key amenities (kitchen, bathrooms, laundry, storage)
- Avoid shooting into mirrors or windows (creates glare)

**Minimum photos:** 10-15 for an online listing. More is better.

## Showing Logistics

### Individual Showings
- Confirm the appointment 24 hours before
- Arrive 15-30 minutes early to open up and turn on lights
- Let the prospect walk through at their pace
- Answer questions but don't oversell
- Have applications available (paper or digital link)

### Open House / Group Showings
- Schedule 1-2 hour window (e.g., Saturday 10am-12pm)
- Advertise the open house in your listing
- More efficient for high-demand properties
- Creates urgency (prospects see competition)

### Self-Showing System
- See the self-showing option in `templates/checklists/pre-screening-funnel.md`
- Pre-qualify by phone before providing access
- Change the code after each showing
