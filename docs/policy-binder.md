# Policy Binder

**Purpose:** Single reference document for how you run your rental business. Every policy is written down so you can point to it when enforcing rules. This gives you a "third party to blame" - instead of "I won't let you," it's "the policy says."
**Key principle:** Consistent application of written policies protects you legally and makes enforcement easier emotionally.

---

## Payment Policies

### Rent
- Rent is due on the **1st of each month**
- Grace period: **[X] days** (rent received after the [X]th is late)
- Accepted payment methods: [Zelle / check / money order / online portal]
- Cash is **never accepted** (no exceptions)
- Rent must be paid in full. Partial payments are not accepted unless a signed payment plan agreement is in place.

### Late Fees
- Late fee: **$[Amount]** assessed on day [X+1]
- Daily late fee: **$[Amount] per day** beginning day [X+1]
- Late fees are never waived. Consistent enforcement is required for fair housing compliance.
- All payment arrangements require a signed promissory note (see `templates/forms/payment-plan-agreement.md`)

### Move-In Funds
- All move-in funds must be in **guaranteed form** (money order or cashier's check)
- No personal checks accepted for move-in
- Full security deposit collected before keys are provided
- No installment plans for security deposits

## Maintenance Policies

### How to Submit a Request
- All non-emergency maintenance requests must be submitted [in writing / via email / via online portal]
- Phone calls are accepted for **emergencies only** (see emergency definitions below)
- Include: description of the issue, photos if possible, best times for access

### Response Times
- **Emergency** (water leak, no heat, gas smell, electrical hazard, fire, security breach): Immediate response
- **Urgent** (only toilet broken, fridge not working, door won't lock, active roof leak): Within 24 hours
- **Routine** (dripping faucet, minor cosmetic, appliance issue): Scheduled within 3-7 business days

### Tenant Responsibilities
- Report any leaks or drips within **48 hours**. Unreported damage that causes further damage is the tenant's financial responsibility.
- Replace HVAC filters [monthly / quarterly / as specified in lease]
- Keep the unit in clean and sanitary condition
- Do not attempt repairs yourself unless explicitly authorized
- Never withhold rent for maintenance issues. Follow the formal request process.

## Guest and Occupancy Policy

- **All adults (18+) residing in the unit must be named on the lease.**
- Guests may stay for up to **14 consecutive days** or **30 total days per year** without being added to the lease.
- Anyone staying beyond these limits must submit a rental application and be approved before continuing to reside in the unit.
- Unauthorized occupants are a lease violation (see `templates/comms/notice-to-comply.md`)

## Entry and Inspection Policy

- The owner/property manager will provide **[state-required notice period, e.g., 24 hours]** written notice before entering the property for non-emergency reasons.
- Entry will occur during reasonable hours: **[e.g., 8 AM - 6 PM, Monday-Saturday]**
- **Emergency entry** (imminent threat to life, safety, or property) does not require advance notice.
- Routine inspections are conducted **[semi-annually / annually]**
- Every maintenance visit also serves as a property inspection. The maintenance worker will document the general condition of the unit.

## Lease Violation Policy

1. **First violation (non-safety):** Written notice to comply with cure period per state law (see `templates/comms/notice-to-comply.md`)
2. **Second violation (same issue):** Written notice with shorter cure period (if permitted by state law)
3. **Ongoing non-compliance:** Begin formal proceedings per `compliance/eviction.md`
4. **Safety violations:** Immediate notice; shortened cure period per state law

All violations are documented with photos where applicable.
