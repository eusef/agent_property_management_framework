**When to use:** Annual review of existing properties. Due diligence on potential purchases. See also `templates/checklists/annual-review.md` and `templates/checklists/property-acquisition.md`.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value | Calculation |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Net Operating Income (NOI)** | $ | Effective Gross Income - Total Operating Expenses |
| **Debt Service (mortgage)** | $ /month, $ /year | Principal + Interest (P&I) |
| **Cash Flow (before tax)** | $ /month, $ /year | NOI - Debt Service |
| **Cash-on-Cash Return** | ____% | Annual Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested x 100 |
| **Cap Rate** | ____% | NOI / Property Value x 100 |
| **Operating Expense Ratio** | ____% | Total Operating Expenses / Effective Gross Income x 100 |

---

## Cap Rate Benchmarks

| Market Type | Typical Cap Rate |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Strong urban market | ~6% |
| Typical suburban market | 8-9% |
| Rural / higher-risk market | 10-12% |

A higher cap rate means higher return but usually higher risk.
