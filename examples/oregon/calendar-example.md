# Oregon Compliance Calendar (Example)

**Purpose:** Example of a filled-in compliance calendar for Oregon rental properties. Use this as a reference when completing your own `compliance/calendar.md`.
**Note:** This is a template showing what categories and deadlines apply in Oregon. Replace example dates and property names with your own.

## Oregon-Specific Deadlines to Remember

| Rule | Deadline | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Security deposit return | 31 days after move-out | ORS 90.300. Must include itemized statement. |
| Rent increase notice | 90 days before effective date | ORS 90.323. In writing. |
| No-cause termination notice | 90 days | ORS 90.427. Plus relocation assistance in Portland. |
| Non-payment notice | 10 days to cure | ORS 90.394 |
| Lease violation notice | 14 days to cure / 30 days to vacate | ORS 90.392 |
| Entry notice | 24 hours written notice | ORS 90.322 |
