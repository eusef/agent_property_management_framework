# Move-In Inspection Checklist

**When to use:** Complete with tenant present on move-in day, before handing over keys. Both parties sign. This is your baseline for security deposit deductions at move-out.
**Oregon law note:** While Oregon doesn't mandate a move-in inspection, it's critical for deposit disputes. Without documentation, courts often side with the tenant.
