# Tenant Screening Checklist

**When to use:** For every applicant being considered for tenancy. Apply the same criteria to every applicant to comply with fair housing laws.
**Oregon law note:** You may charge a screening fee, but it cannot exceed actual costs (ORS 90.295). You must provide a receipt. Oregon bans discrimination based on source of income (Section 8 vouchers must be accepted).
**Fair housing:** Apply identical criteria to all applicants. Document your criteria before you start screening. See compliance/fair-housing.md.
