# Move-In Day Procedures Checklist

**When to use:** On move-in day, in this exact sequence. Do not hand over keys until every item is completed.
**Key principle:** The first interaction sets the tone for the entire tenancy. Be professional, thorough, and firm on the process.

## Before Tenant Arrives

- [ ] Unit is clean and ready (turnover checklist completed - see `templates/checklists/unit-turnover.md`)
- [ ] All systems tested and working (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, appliances)
- [ ] Smoke and CO detectors tested with fresh batteries
- [ ] Keys cut and counted: _____ house keys, _____ mailbox keys, _____ garage openers
- [ ] Move-in inspection form printed (see `templates/checklists/move-in-inspection.md`)
- [ ] Welcome packet prepared (see `templates/comms/move-in-welcome.md`)
- [ ] Camera / phone ready for move-in photos

---

---

## Step 2: Sign the Lease (Before Keys)

- [ ] Lease reviewed with tenant (key terms highlighted)
- [ ] All adults who will reside in the unit are named on the lease
- [ ] Lease addendums signed:
  - [ ] Pet addendum (if applicable)
  - [ ] Fee schedule addendum
  - [ ] Rules and regulations addendum
  - [ ] Lead-based paint disclosure (if property built before 1978)
  - [ ] Other: _____________
- [ ] Both parties have signed all documents
- [ ] Tenant received a complete copy of the signed lease

**Do NOT proceed to Step 3 until the lease is fully executed.**

---

## Step 3: Disclosures and Documentation

- [ ] Lead-based paint disclosure and EPA pamphlet provided (pre-1978 properties)
- [ ] Move-in welcome packet provided (see `templates/comms/move-in-welcome.md`)
- [ ] Emergency procedures reviewed (water shutoff location, gas shutoff, electrical panel)
- [ ] Maintenance request process explained (how to submit, emergency vs. routine)
- [ ] Renter's insurance proof collected
  - Carrier: _____________
  - Policy #: _____________
  - Expiration: _____________
  - [ ] Recorded in `compliance/insurance-tracker.md`
- [ ] 48-hour leak/drip reporting clause reviewed (tenant must report leaks within 48 hours; unreported damage that causes further damage is tenant's responsibility)

---

## Step 4: Property Walkthrough and Inspection

- [ ] Move-in inspection completed with tenant present (see `templates/checklists/move-in-inspection.md`)
- [ ] All rooms photographed with timestamps
- [ ] Tenant signed the move-in inspection form
- [ ] Photos saved to `properties/<property>/inspections/move-in-[DATE]/`

## Step 6: Final Items
