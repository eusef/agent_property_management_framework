## 2. Property Due Diligence

- [ ] Property inspection completed (date: _______)
  - [ ] Roof condition and estimated remaining life
  - [ ] Foundation - any cracks or settling
  - [ ] Plumbing - material, age, condition
  - [ ] Electrical - panel capacity, wiring type
  - [ ] HVAC - age, condition, estimated replacement cost
  - [ ] Water heater - age, condition
  - [ ] Windows - condition, energy efficiency
  - [ ] Siding/exterior - condition
  - [ ] Pest/termite inspection
- [ ] Title search completed - any liens, easements, encumbrances?
- [ ] Survey reviewed (if available)
- [ ] Environmental concerns (flood zone, lead paint, asbestos, radon)
- [ ] Year built: _______ (pre-1978 = lead paint disclosure required)

## 3. Market Analysis

- [ ] Comparable rents pulled (min 3 comps within 1 mile)
- [ ] Vacancy rate in the area
- [ ] Neighborhood trend (improving, stable, declining)
- [ ] School district rating (affects tenant demand)
- [ ] Proximity to employment centers
- [ ] Any planned development nearby (positive or negative)

## 4. Legal / Regulatory

- [ ] Zoning allows rental use
- [ ] Any HOA restrictions on renting
- [ ] City/county rental registration required?
- [ ] Any rent control applicability (Oregon: buildings 15+ years old)
- [ ] Business license required?
- [ ] Lead paint disclosure required? (pre-1978)

## 5. Insurance

- [ ] Landlord insurance quote obtained: $___/year
- [ ] Liability coverage adequate
- [ ] Flood insurance needed? (check FEMA maps)
- [ ] Umbrella policy covers this property?

## 6. Financing

- [ ] Pre-approval obtained
- [ ] Down payment available: $___
- [ ] Closing costs estimated: $___
- [ ] Cash reserves after closing: $___
