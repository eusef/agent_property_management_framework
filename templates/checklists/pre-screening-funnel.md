# Pre-Screening Funnel

**When to use:** Before formal screening begins. This filters out unqualified prospects before they fill out an application, saving time and reducing fair housing risk.
**Key principle:** The earlier you filter, the less time wasted on unqualified applicants.

## Step 2: Phone / Email Pre-Screen Script

When a prospect contacts you, ask these questions before scheduling a showing. These are objective, non-discriminatory qualification questions.

**Opening:** "Thanks for your interest in [Property Address]. Let me give you a quick overview and ask a few questions to make sure it's a good fit."

**Questions:**

1. "When are you looking to move in?"
   - Answer: _____________ (Does it match your availability?)

2. "How many people would be living in the unit?"
   - Answer: _____________ (Does it comply with occupancy standards?)

3. "The rent is $[Amount] per month. We require verifiable gross income of at least 3x that amount, so $[3x Amount]. Does that work for your household?"
   - Answer: [ ] Yes [ ] No

4. "Do you have any pets?" (if applicable)
   - Answer: _____________ (Matches your pet policy?)

5. "Have you ever been evicted or broken a lease?"
   - Answer: _____________

6. "Are you willing to submit to a credit and background check as part of the application?"
   - Answer: [ ] Yes [ ] No

**Outcome:**
- [ ] Passes pre-screen - schedule showing
- [ ] Does not meet minimum criteria - thank them and move on
- [ ] Needs follow-up: _____________

**Note:** Apply these same questions to every prospect. Never add or skip questions based on any protected class characteristic.

## Self-Showing Option

For landlords managing multiple properties or living far from the rental, a self-showing system reduces time investment significantly:

1. Install a lockbox or smart lock with temporary codes
2. Set up a dedicated Google Voice number for the property
3. Pre-qualify prospects by phone (Step 2 above) before giving access
4. Provide a temporary access code with a specific time window
5. Follow up within 24 hours to answer questions and offer the application

**Benefits:** Eliminates scheduling conflicts, allows prospects to view on their own schedule, and filters out low-interest inquiries (serious prospects will make the effort).

**Risk mitigation:** Never give access without completing the phone pre-screen first. Change the code after each showing.
