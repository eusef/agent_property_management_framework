# Maintenance Status Update

**When to use:** After scheduling a contractor, after work is completed, or when there's a delay. Keep tenants informed at every stage.
**Best practice:** Tenants should never have to ask "what's happening with my repair?" Proactive updates prevent frustration.

---

## Contractor Scheduled

Hi [Tenant Name],

Update on your [maintenance issue]:

A [contractor type] is scheduled to come on [Date] between [Time Range]. They'll need access to [specific area].

Please let me know if that time doesn't work and I'll reschedule.

Phil Johnston

---

## Work Completed

Hi [Tenant Name],

The [repair description] has been completed. [Brief summary of what was done.]

Please let me know if everything looks good or if you notice any remaining issues.

Thanks,
Phil Johnston

---

## Delay Notification

Hi [Tenant Name],

I wanted to give you an update on the [maintenance issue]. Unfortunately, [reason for delay - parts on order, contractor scheduling, etc.].

The new estimated timeline is [updated timeframe]. I apologize for the inconvenience and will keep you posted.

[If applicable: In the meantime, [any interim solution or workaround].]

Phil Johnston
