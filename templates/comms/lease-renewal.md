# Lease Renewal Notice

**When to use:** 90+ days before lease expiration (Oregon requires 90 days notice for rent increases on qualifying properties).
**Oregon law note:** For buildings 15+ years old, rent increases are capped at 7% + CPI per year (Oregon rent control, ORS 90.323). Check if your property qualifies before proposing an increase.
**Timing:** Send at least 90 days before current lease end date, even if not changing rent.

---

## Renewal Offer - Same Terms

Subject: Lease renewal for [Property Address]

Hi [Tenant Name],

Your current lease at [Property Address] expires on [Expiration Date]. I'd like to offer you a renewal on the same terms:

- Monthly rent: $[Amount]
- Lease term: [12 months / month-to-month]
- New lease dates: [Start Date] to [End Date]

If you'd like to renew, please let me know by [Response Deadline - at least 30 days before expiration]. If I don't hear back, your lease will convert to month-to-month per Oregon law.

Thanks for being a great tenant.

Phil Johnston

## Renewal Offer - With Rent Adjustment

Subject: Lease renewal for [Property Address]

Hi [Tenant Name],

Your current lease at [Property Address] expires on [Expiration Date]. I'd like to offer you a renewal with the following terms:

- Monthly rent: $[New Amount] (currently $[Current Amount])
- Lease term: [12 months / month-to-month]
- New lease dates: [Start Date] to [End Date]

This adjustment reflects [brief rationale - market conditions, property improvements, increased costs, etc.]. Comparable units in the area are currently renting for $[Comp Range].

Please let me know by [Response Deadline] if you'd like to renew. I'm happy to discuss.

Phil Johnston

## Non-Renewal Notice

Subject: Lease non-renewal notice for [Property Address]

Hi [Tenant Name],

This is to inform you that I will not be renewing the lease at [Property Address], which expires on [Expiration Date]. Per Oregon law, this serves as your [90-day / applicable period] notice.

Your move-out date is [Date]. I'll send a separate message with move-out procedures and inspection scheduling.

If you have questions about the timeline or process, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Phil Johnston
