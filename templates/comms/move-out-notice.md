# Move-Out Notice and Procedures

**When to use:** After receiving notice from tenant, or after issuing non-renewal. Send move-out procedures at least 30 days before move-out date.
**Oregon law note:** Security deposit must be returned with itemized statement within 31 days of move-out (ORS 90.300).

---

## Move-Out Procedures Letter

Subject: Move-out procedures for [Property Address]

Hi [Tenant Name],

Thank you for the notice that you'll be moving out on [Move-Out Date]. Here's what to expect for a smooth transition:

**Before Move-Out Day**
- Clean the unit thoroughly (see cleaning expectations below)
- Remove all personal belongings and trash
- Patch any nail holes with spackle (small holes only)
- Replace any burned-out light bulbs
- Make sure all smoke/CO detectors are in place and working

**Cleaning Expectations**
- Kitchen: Clean all appliances (oven, fridge, dishwasher), counters, cabinets inside and out
- Bathrooms: Clean toilet, tub/shower, sink, mirrors, floors
- Floors: Vacuum all carpets, sweep and mop hard floors
- Windows: Clean interior glass
- Walls: Wipe down any marks or scuffs

**Move-Out Day**
- Return all keys, garage openers, and access devices to [Phil / lockbox / etc.]
- Final walkthrough: I'll schedule this for [Date/Time]. Your presence is optional but recommended.

**After Move-Out**
- I'll complete the final inspection and compare to your move-in report
- Your security deposit of $[Amount] will be returned within 31 days, along with an itemized statement of any deductions (per Oregon law)
- Deposit refund will be sent to your forwarding address

**Please provide your forwarding address before move-out:** [Email or text it to me at the contact info below]

**Referral Bonus:** If you know someone who would be a great tenant for this property, I offer a $[Amount] referral bonus for qualified referrals who sign a lease. Feel free to share my contact information.

If you have any questions, let me know.

Phil Johnston
[Phone]
[Email]

## Move-Out Workflow Notes (Internal - Do Not Send to Tenant)

**Send this notice twice:**
1. Immediately upon receiving the tenant's vacancy notice
2. Again 7 days before the move-out date (as a reminder)

**Parallel actions upon receiving notice:**
- [ ] Begin advertising the vacancy immediately (see `docs/vacancy-marketing.md`)
- [ ] Schedule the move-out walkthrough
- [ ] Schedule turnover contractors (painting, cleaning, etc.) for the day after move-out
- [ ] Add security deposit return deadline to `compliance/calendar.md`
- [ ] Prepare the unit turnover checklist (see `templates/checklists/unit-turnover.md`)
