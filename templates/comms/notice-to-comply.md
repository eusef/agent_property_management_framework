# Notice to Comply (Cure or Quit)

**When to use:** When a tenant is violating a lease term other than non-payment of rent. Common violations: unauthorized pets, unauthorized occupants, noise complaints, property damage, unapproved modifications, parking violations, cleanliness issues.
**Important:** Check your state law for required notice periods, delivery methods, and cure rights. This template must be customized to your jurisdiction.
