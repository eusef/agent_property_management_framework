# Property Inspection Notice

**When to use:** Before any non-emergency entry to the unit. Oregon requires 24 hours written notice (ORS 90.322).
**Oregon law note:** Landlord may enter for inspections, repairs, or showings with 24-hour written notice during reasonable hours. Emergency entry requires no notice.

---

## Routine Inspection Notice

Subject: Scheduled property inspection - [Date]

Hi [Tenant Name],

This is to let you know that I'll be conducting a routine property inspection at [Property Address] on:

**Date:** [Date]
**Time:** [Time Range, e.g., 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM]

This is a standard [semi-annual / annual] inspection to check the overall condition of the property, including plumbing, HVAC, smoke/CO detectors, and general maintenance needs. It typically takes about [30-45 minutes].

You're welcome to be present but it's not required. [If applicable: I'll have [contractor name] with me to check [specific system].]

If this time doesn't work, please let me know by [date] and I'll reschedule.

Per Oregon law (ORS 90.322), this serves as your 24-hour minimum written notice of entry.

Phil Johnston
