# Adverse Action Notice

**When to use:** Required by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) whenever you deny an applicant based in whole or in part on information from a consumer report (credit report, background check, tenant screening report).
**Important:** Failure to provide this notice is a federal violation. Send within a reasonable time after the denial decision.

---

## Adverse Action Notice Template

**Date:** _______________

**To:**
[Applicant Name]
[Applicant Address]

**Re:** Application for rental at [Property Address]

Dear [Applicant Name],

Thank you for your application to rent the property at [Property Address]. After careful review, we are unable to approve your application at this time.

This decision was based, in whole or in part, on information contained in a consumer report obtained from the following consumer reporting agency:

**Consumer Reporting Agency:**
[Screening Company Name]
[Screening Company Address]
[Screening Company Phone Number]
[Screening Company Website]

Please be advised of the following rights under the Fair Credit Reporting Act:

1. The consumer reporting agency listed above did not make the decision to take this adverse action and is unable to provide specific reasons for it.

2. You have the right to obtain a free copy of your consumer report from the agency listed above within 60 days of receiving this notice.

3. You have the right to dispute the accuracy or completeness of any information in the consumer report directly with the reporting agency.

4. [YOUR_STATE additional rights, if any: _______________]

If you have questions about this notice, you may contact me at the information below.

Sincerely,

[Owner/Property Manager Name]
[Phone]
[Email]

---

## Checklist Before Sending

- [ ] Applicant name and address are correct
- [ ] Screening company name, address, phone, and website are accurate
- [ ] Notice is being sent within a reasonable time of the denial decision
- [ ] A copy of this notice is saved in the applicant's file
- [ ] Denial reason is documented internally (separate from this notice) for fair housing records
- [ ] Notice is sent via a method that provides proof of delivery (email with read receipt, certified mail, or hand delivery with signature)
