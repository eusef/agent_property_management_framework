# Maintenance Request Acknowledgment

**When to use:** Immediately after a tenant reports a maintenance issue (target: within 4 hours of receiving request).
**Why it matters:** Sets expectations, documents the request, and builds trust. Oregon law requires landlords to maintain habitable conditions (ORS 90.320).

---

## Standard Acknowledgment

Hi [Tenant Name],

Thank you for letting me know about [brief description of issue]. I've logged this and will be working on getting it resolved.

Here's what to expect next:
- I'll assess the situation and arrange for a [contractor type] to take a look
- I'll follow up with a scheduled time within [24-48 hours / timeframe]
- If you notice the issue getting worse before then, please contact me right away at [Phone]

[If applicable: In the meantime, please [any interim safety/mitigation steps].]

Thanks for your patience.

Phil Johnston
[Phone]
[Email]

---

## Emergency Acknowledgment

Hi [Tenant Name],

I received your report about [emergency issue]. This is being treated as an emergency.

[I've already contacted / I'm contacting] a [contractor type] and will get someone out as quickly as possible - ideally within [timeframe].

[If applicable: For your safety, please [specific instructions - e.g., turn off water main, do not use affected area, etc.].]

I'll keep you updated. If you need to reach me urgently, call [Phone] directly.

Phil Johnston
