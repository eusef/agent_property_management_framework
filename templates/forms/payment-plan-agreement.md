# Payment Plan Agreement (Promissory Note)

**When to use:** When a tenant is unable to pay rent in full and you agree to a structured payment plan. All payment arrangements MUST be in writing. Never make verbal payment agreements.
**Important:** Do NOT enter into a payment plan if eviction proceedings have already started. Accepting partial payment during eviction resets the process in most states.

### Terms and Conditions

1. All payments must be made via [accepted payment methods] by the dates specified above.
2. If any payment is missed or late, the entire remaining balance becomes due immediately.
3. If any payment is missed or late, the Landlord may proceed with legal remedies including eviction without further notice beyond what is required by state law.
4. This agreement does not waive any rights of the Landlord under the lease or applicable law.
5. Late fees continue to apply per the lease agreement for any payments not received by the specified dates.
6. This agreement supersedes any prior verbal discussions about payment arrangements.
