# Fee Schedule Template

**When to use:** Customize this for your properties and include as a lease addendum. All fees must be disclosed in the lease agreement to be enforceable. Check your state law for fee limits and requirements.
**Key principle:** Fees serve two purposes: (1) recover costs for tenant-caused issues, and (2) incentivize compliance with lease terms.

---

## Standard Fee Schedule

Customize the amounts below based on your market, state law, and lease terms.

### Rent-Related Fees

| Fee | Amount | Trigger | Notes |
|-----|--------|---------|-------|
| Late fee | $_____ or _____% of rent | Rent not received by grace period day | Must be specified in lease. Check state limits. |
| Daily late fee | $_____ per day | Each day past grace period | Some states cap this. Verify. |
| Returned check / failed payment | $_____ | NSF or reversed electronic payment | Typically $25-50. Cover your bank's fee plus admin time. |

### Lease Violation Fees

| Fee | Amount | Trigger | Notes |
|-----|--------|---------|-------|
| Unauthorized pet | $_____ | Pet found on premises not on lease | After notice to comply. Not applicable to service/support animals. |
| Unauthorized occupant | $_____ | Person living in unit not on lease | After notice to comply. |
| Smoking violation | $_____ | Smoking in non-smoking unit | If smoke-free policy is in lease. |
| Noise / disturbance | $_____ | After written warning | Document each incident. |

### Maintenance-Related Fees

| Fee | Amount | Trigger | Notes |
|-----|--------|---------|-------|
| Dirty HVAC filter at inspection | $_____ | Filter not changed per lease requirement | Provide replacement filters or specify tenant responsibility in lease. |
| Clogged drain (tenant-caused) | $_____ | Grease, hair, foreign objects | Plumber invoice passed through. |
| Lightbulb replacement (if lease requires tenant to maintain) | $_____ each | Burned out bulbs at inspection | Only if lease assigns this to tenant. |
| Missed maintenance appointment | $_____ | Tenant not present for scheduled repair | 24-hour notice was provided. |
| Lock change (tenant request, non-emergency) | $_____ | Tenant requests re-key | Actual cost of locksmith. |

### Move-Related Fees

| Fee | Amount | Trigger | Notes |
|-----|--------|---------|-------|
| Early lease termination | $_____ or _____ months' rent | Tenant breaks lease before term | Must be specified in lease. Check state limits. |
| Holdover rent premium | _____% above normal rent | Tenant stays past lease end without approval | Check state law on holdover tenancy. |

### Pet Fees (If Pets Allowed)

| Fee | Amount | Trigger | Notes |
|-----|--------|---------|-------|
| Monthly pet rent | $_____ per animal | Ongoing | Not applicable to service/support animals. |
| Non-refundable pet fee | $_____ one-time | At move-in | Some states prohibit non-refundable fees. Verify. |
| Pet deposit (refundable) | $_____ | At move-in | Returned minus damages. Some states include in deposit cap. |

---

## Important Notes

1. **Service and support animals are NOT pets.** You cannot charge pet fees, pet rent, or pet deposits for service animals or emotional support animals. Doing so is a fair housing violation.

2. **All fees must be in the lease.** A fee not specified in the lease is likely unenforceable. Include this schedule as a lease addendum signed by the tenant.

3. **Fees must be "reasonable."** Courts in most states will not enforce fees that appear punitive rather than compensatory. Set amounts that reflect actual costs or reasonable estimates.

4. **Track all fee income in income.csv** using the appropriate category (late_fee, pet_deposit, application_fee, other).

5. **Never waive fees without documenting the reason.** Consistent enforcement prevents fair housing complaints and tenant expectations of exceptions.
